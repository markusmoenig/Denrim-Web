## A complete low-poly workflow

Build game props, stylized figurines, and printable assets with object, vertex, edge, and face editing; snapping and transform gizmos; primitives and direct mesh-editing tools; materials and texture painting; and dedicated Edit and Render modes.

Projects include thumbnails, backups, undo and redo, plus export to OBJ, USDZ, STL, GLB, and rendered PNG.

## Localized from day one

The interface, action descriptions, status messages, tooltips, and in-app help are available in English, German, Spanish, French, Japanese, Korean, and Simplified Chinese.
