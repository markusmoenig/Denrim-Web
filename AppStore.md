Procedural Noise & Textures

Description
Transform noise into art. Create procedural textures, seamless patterns, and vivid backgrounds with professional-grade tools.
- 7 noise types: OpenSimplex / OpenSimplex+, Perlin, Value, Cellular (Voronoi), Sinusoidal, and Gabor
- Fractals: FBM, Ridged, Ping Pong (up to 8 octaves)
- Domain warping for flowing, organic effects
- Seamless tiling (1×1 to 4×4) for games, 3D, and motion graphics
- 4-color gradients with contrast/brightness controls
- 3D preview with bump/normal-style lighting and material controls
- Export 512–8192px (PNG/JPEG) with common aspect ratios or custom sizes
- Undo/redo and iCloud sync
- Universal purchase for Mac and iPad

Perfect for game devs, 3D artists, digital artists, web designers, and anyone needing tileable textures or wallpapers.
